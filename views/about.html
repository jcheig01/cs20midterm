Our Mission

"One small step for you, one big for human kind"

This company was started by four college students with an out-of-this-world vision for the future of street wear shoes. We imagined a world where shoes are not only of the highest quality and artistry, but also contribute to a good cause.

That is why for each shoe you purchase, we donate 50% of the profit directly to NASA and Space-X. As featured on the Science Magazine, Elon Musk's first gift to his baby mama, Grimes, was a pair of moon shoes. We believe that space travel is a basic human right, and with each pair of shoes you buy, we are one step closer to achieving extraterrestrial colonization.
