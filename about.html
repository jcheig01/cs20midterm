Our Mission

"One small step for you, one big for human kind"

This company was started by four college students with an out of this world vision for the future of street wear shoes. We imagined a world where shoes are not only of the highest quality and artistry, but also contribute to a good cause.

That is why for each shoe you donate 95% of all profit is given directly to NASA and Space-X. As fitured in Science Magazine. Elon Musk's first gift to his baby mama, Grimes, was a pair of Moon Shoes. We believe that space travel is a basic human right, and with each pair shoes you buy, we are ont step closer to achieving extraterrestrial colonization.
